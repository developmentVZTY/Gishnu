Product Specifications

Our Aerator Gearboxes are engineered to deliver reliable performance in demanding industrial environments such as wastewater treatment, biogas plants, and aeration basins. Each unit is crafted with precision components and heavy-duty materials to ensure long-term durability and low maintenance

Feature Details
Gear Ratio Available up to 50:1, offering torque multiplication and speed control suited for heavy aeration loads.
Torque Capacity Handles torque up to 10,000 Nm, suitable for large impellers and high-resistance environments.
Mounting Options Foot-mounted and flange-mounted options available, compatible with both horizontal and vertical installations.
Material Grade Constructed using EN GJSâ€‘600 ductile iron for superior tensile strength and resistance to corrosion and wear.
Sealing Double-lip oil seals and labyrinth construction to prevent ingress of water and dust in moist environments.
Bearings Heavy-duty tapered roller bearings to withstand radial and axial loads continuously.
Lubrication Factory-filled with synthetic oil (grade VG 220/320) for extended service intervals.
Customization Units can be tailored for shaft size, mounting configuration, paint coating, and gear profiles based on application needs.
