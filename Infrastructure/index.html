INFRASTRUCTURE

Our sprawling infrastructural facility is spread across an area of 50,000 sq. ft in a span of 3 different units. We are well-equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and highly-configured machines that are ideal for manufacturing high-precision, durable and quality products. Our integrated manufacturing facility is divided into units that comprise design & development, manufacturing, quality checking and delivery departments. We are able to meet the ever-growing demand of our clients with the help of our highly skilled technical team and in-house design team. Our dedicated technocrats are well-versed with the changing demands of the industry, and thus keep their ears and eyes open to new processes. We are equipped with the following machinery:

  • Hoffler Helix 400 & Klinglenberg P40
  • Nimble NOAH 400H – CNC Gear Hobbing
  • CMM- Accurate Spectra
  • Nidec ST25 Gear Shaper
  • Brinell hardness Tester KB300H
  • Rockwell Hardness Tester
  • CNC turning machine
  • Hobbing machine
  • Thread milling machine
  • Worm grinding machine
  • Horizontal broaching machine
  • All types of gauges and instruments
